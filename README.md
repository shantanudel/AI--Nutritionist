🥗 AI Nutritionist
# 📌 OVERVIEW: AI Nutritionist is a machine learning-powered dietary assistant that provides personalized nutrition recommendations based on user input. The system analyzes eating habits, nutritional needs, and health goals to generate smart meal suggestions and actionable insights.

Designed with a modular and scalable architecture, the project aims to simplify daily diet planning and promote healthier lifestyle choices through intelligent automation.

# Features
AI-Driven Personalization
Leverages advanced models like Gemini Pro to analyze user preferences, health conditions, and fitness goals, delivering highly tailored nutrition recommendations.

Intelligent Meal Planning Generates customized, nutritionally balanced meal plans aligned with specific objectives such as weight management, diabetes control, and muscle development.

Seamless Food Logging Enables effortless meal tracking through photo recognition and barcode scanning for quick and accurate input.

Real-Time Nutritional Insights Provides instant feedback on calorie intake, macronutrient distribution, and overall meal quality to support informed dietary decisions.

Fitness Integration Synchronizes with wearable devices to incorporate activity data, ensuring more precise and adaptive recommendations.

Health & Wellness Resources Offers curated educational content, including articles, tips, and guides to promote long-term, sustainable healthy living.

# Real-Life Scenarios
Weight Loss Journey: Emma, a vegetarian, uses the app to follow a personalized calorie-controlled plan and track progress.

Diabetes Management: George receives low-carb meal suggestions and carb/glycemic index insights to manage Type 2 Diabetes.

Muscle Building: Rose, a strength-training enthusiast, follows high-protein meal plans aligned with her workout data.
